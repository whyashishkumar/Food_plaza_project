How to Get Here

From Kashmiri Gate Inter State Bus Terminal (ISBT)
FOOD PLAZA is approximately 33 km from Kashmiri gate ISBT. Approximate fare of taxi from ISBT Kashmiri gate to FOOD PLAZA is around Rs. 550-700. One can board bus bound for Meerut from Kashmiri gate ISBT. Bus will pass through the FOOD PLAZA. Approximate fare for bus travelling is Rs. 60-80.
Directions from Railway Station
FFrom Ghaziabad railway station, FOOD PLAZA is around 18 km. One can board auto or taxi from Ghaziabad, railway station
