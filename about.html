HOW WE'VE STARTED OUR RESTAURANT

WE ARE FOOD PLAZA.

We are a hopeful harbinger vivifying compilation of family recipes, lovingly contributed by our world-class chefs, friends and family. Here we hope to reinvent and cajole the story of these authentic recipes, who inspired them to cook it and how they have tweaked it today due to changing tastes, ingredients and techniques. We try and recreate the smell, taste and the memory of the dish, fondly remembered, into a recipe to be shared. With the sharing of these recipes and the stories behind them, we want to connect with people from far and wide through a common language, food.

Even with the new trends in the culinary world, when talk turns to food, most people remember fondly, food cooked by their grandmothers, mothers, aunts, and though they remember the taste and the love that went into cooking the dish. We hope to reimburse those lost food memories Let’s recreate the love, taste and story behind every pinch of salt, every sprinkle of nostalgia and every brisk of delicious chronicles.

COME WITH US ON THIS REJUVENATING EXPERIENCE. WE AWAIT YOUR PRESENCE.
